### Objective

Enable musicians to create, manage, and consult songs in a clear and practical way,
with a strong focus on chords, song structure, and transposition.

Bandmate MVP v0 is designed as a **personal tool for musicians**, prioritizing usability
during practice, rehearsal, and composition.

---

### Included in MVP

- Manual song creation
- Song structure by sections (verse, chorus, bridge, etc.)
- Clear and readable chord visualization
- Section-based navigation
- Chord transposition
- Personal / local usage

---

### Not Included (for now)

- Public sharing of songs
- Public song catalog
- PDF export
- Advanced practice mode (metronome, loops, etc.)
- Harmonic or compositional suggestions
- Copyright management or public content handling

---

### Success Criteria

- A musician can load or create a song and play it comfortably
- The application is usable on mobile during rehearsals
- Navigation and chord readability do not get in the way of making music
